## Hello.


I'm Nick Woods, a Product Design Leader with 20 years experience scaling teams and products across global markets. Currently Head of Product at Polycam, where I've grown our product, design, and research team from 2 to 6 people while we grew our customer base from 30k to 115k+, driving 650% ARR growth. I've worked across agencies, startups, and corporations from Nike to Microsoft, always bringing a maker's mindset to leadership challenges.


‍**My philosophy:**
I'm a Product Design Leader who scales through making. Player/Coach is part of my DNA. That could mean highly polished visual design, prototyping and UX, facilitating design thinking, mapping service design flows, user research, or managing people and leading teams. I love messy processes, efficient outcomes, and solving real world problems with emerging technology. I'm a fluid adopter who integrates new tools thoughtfully rather than chasing trends.
